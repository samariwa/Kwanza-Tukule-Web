KWANZA TUKULE














Cashless

There is no physical money handled in the chain.

B2B

Kwanza tukule benefits vendors which trickles down to consumer.






Mobile-based

Kwanza tukule uses technology to automate the chain.

Sourcing

We source all food ingredients directly from the farmers cutting the cost of the middle men and ensuring consistency in quality and mostly during the harvest season then using the latest technology to preserve the raw ingredients.

Nutrition

We supply high quality and nutritious pre-prepare foods to street vendors to address the problem of undernourishment especially among the low income earners in Kenya by ensuring that they server this to customers.

Hygiene

Street food vendors serve food to 84% of the urban Kenyan population.
Our goal is to support street food vendors in their efforts to supply the Kenyan working population with hygienic food prepared in the most utmost hygienic conditions.

Energy

We believe in energy smart food production and processing.
Green energy makes sustainable food production possible.
Our goal is to help street food vendors use green energy in the food preparation process.
